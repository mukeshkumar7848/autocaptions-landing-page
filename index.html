Process

From audio to captions
in four simple steps.

01

Install & Setup

Install the extension, open it in After Effects, and select the Toolkit folder. One-time setup takes under 2 minutes.

02

Configure Settings

Choose language (or auto-detect), model size, position, font size, and caption options. Everything runs locally on your machine.

03

Generate Captions

Click "Generate Captions" and the AI transcribes your audio into perfectly timed text layers directly in your comp.

04

Animate & Export

Apply animation presets (Fade, Bounce, Slide, etc.), add background boxes, tweak text, and render. Captions are native AE layers ‚Äî fully editable.
